Welcome to the PIONEER Lab at New Jersey Institute of Technology!

The PIONEER Lab advances human-centered interfaces that empower people with diverse abilities to effectively interact with robots across a range of hardware and autonomy levels. Our research focuses on designing versatile control paradigms, adapting robot support based on user workload, and developing structured training protocols to ensure reliable and consistent performance. By integrating intuitive interaction, intelligent autonomy, and immersive communication, we aim to foster seamless human–robot symbiosis in healthcare, daily living, and other real-world environments.
